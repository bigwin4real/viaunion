Quick access

Verify current terms before purchase

Some discounts require a VIA employee badge, company email, union affiliation, corporate code, or phone booking. Offers can change; use these instructions as a starting point and confirm before paying.

Union Savings

Union discount platform for offers from participating companies.

  1. Open Union Savings.
  2. Choose UNIFOR as your affiliated union.
  3. Select your local, create your account, then browse offers.
Open Union Savings
